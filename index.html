Counter — Privacy Policy

Package: com.countify.app

🔒
This app collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted, shared, or sold.

1. Overview

Counter ("the App") is a tally counter application developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) the App handles, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that information.

By installing or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App.

2. Information Collected

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

Data Purpose Storage Shared?
Counter names & values Restore your counters between sessions Device only Never
Counter history (action log) Display history screen Device only Never
App settings (theme, font, button style) Remember your preferences Device only Never
Background color selection Restore your chosen background Device only Never

All data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences — a standard on-device key-value store. No database, no cloud sync, no server.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID, Android ID)
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Camera, microphone, or media access
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • IP address or any network metadata
  • Contacts, calendar, or files on your device
  • Payment or financial information

4. Internet Access & Permissions

The App does not request the INTERNET permission. It cannot make network requests of any kind. The App functions entirely offline.

The App requests zero Android runtime permissions. No dialogs asking for access to your camera, storage, location, microphone, or contacts will ever appear.

5. Third-Party Libraries

The App uses the following open-source libraries. None of them collect user data:

  • Provider — state management (MIT License)
  • shared_preferences — local storage (BSD License)
  • google_fonts — typography, fonts bundled as assets in release builds — no network calls (Apache 2.0)
  • audioplayers — local sound playback only (MIT License)

No advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting tools are included.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored only on your device and only for as long as the App is installed. You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Using "Clear History" inside the App (Settings screen)
  • Uninstalling the App — this permanently removes all stored data
  • Clearing app data via Android Settings → Apps → Counter → Storage → Clear Data

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the App is safe for all age groups.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the App is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the "Last Updated" date below will be revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
